Working Together

About Me:

I am a Brighton based creative website designer, graphics designer and copy writer. I can offer you a full web design package from conception of brief, guidance on development of content, design of branding and website, advice on post-production marketing, and ongoing hosting and maintenance. As a freelancer I am able to keep my overheads minimal and my prices low, and I can offer you the opportunity to pay for your project by easy monthly installments.

Working With You:

I work with individuals and SMEs to develop beautifully effective websites. I believe good communication is key to good design and development. I like to work collaboratively, and your ideas and feedback are central to that. I can offer in-depth analysis of your existing branding, website and online marketing strategies as a starting point for our project, or together we can paint a blank canvas. Either way I will spend time getting to know you and your company and conceptualize a vision and a product that works for you.
