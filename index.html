About me

Here's me:

I am a Brighton based creative designer. If I had to put myself in a box it would probably be labelled "dreamer, lover of nature and all round life enthusiast". But frankly I don't wish to be contained in box. I like to be free to run and dance and create. That is basically me in a nutshell - I'm ok with being put in a nutshell!

What I can do for you:

I am a friendly reliable and creative web and branding designer and developer. I like to work collaboratively, listening to your ideas and feedback. I can offer analysis of your existing branding, website and online marketing strategies. Through getting to know you and your company, together we can identify the most fitting design strategies. My subjectivity helps me to provide constructive feedback on your ideas. I will make sure you are kept informed throughout the development cycle, inviting feedback and listening to your needs.
