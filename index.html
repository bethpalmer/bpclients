About me

Here's me:

I am a Brighton based creative designer. If I had to put myself in a box it would probably be labelled "dreamer, lover of nature and all round life enthusiast". But frankly I don't wish to be contained in box. I like to be free to run and dance and create and revel in beauty.

What I can do for you:

I am friendly, reliable and skillful. I believe good communication is key to good design and development. I like to work collaboratively, and your ideas and feedback are central to that. I can offer in-depth analysis of your existing branding, website and online marketing strategies as a starting point for our project, or together we can paint a blank canvas. Either way I will spend time getting to know you and your company and conceptualize a vision and a product that works for you.
